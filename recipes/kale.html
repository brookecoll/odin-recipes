Oven Baked Kale

Oven roasted kale is a low effort but high nutrition alterative to conventional chips, or other salty crunchy treats. Enjoy as a snack or as a base to vegetable bowl, the possibilities are endless.

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Wash and Dry kale.
  3. Remove the stem, and chop.
  4. Place kale on a baking sheet and evenly cover with olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and bake for 10 to 15 minutes until crispy.

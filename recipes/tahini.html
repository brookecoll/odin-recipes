Tahini Dressing

Finished Tahini Dressing

Tahini dressing is a fast, easy, and simple dressing that will upgrade any basic roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • salt
  • cumin
  • 6 Tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and combine with lemon juice. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Remove remaining garlic.
  3. Add tahini, a dash of cumin, and salt to preference. Whisk until blended.
  4. Add water a tablespoon at a time, whisking after each addition. Stop at desired consistency, add additional water if a more liquid dressing is desired.

Roasted Chickpeas

Roasted chickpeas are a great low prep, and low effort addition to any salad. These can even be eaten as a side with dressing, or a snack by themselves providing lots of fiber and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups chickpeas
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Make sure chickpeas are dry and lay out evenly on a backing sheet.
  3. Cover in olive oil and salt to taste.
  4. Roast in oven for 30 minutes.
